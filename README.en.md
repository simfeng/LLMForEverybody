## What LearnLLM.AI Offers

**Curated LLM interview questions**: practical questions from the basics to frontier topics, useful if you are preparing for interviews or trying to spot the next career opening.

**Structured paper reading**: a guided path that starts with the 2017 Transformer paper and follows the technical evolution of LLMs in a way that works for developers with different backgrounds.
